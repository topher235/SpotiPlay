About

SpotiPlay is a personal project of mine. I have a passion for music and web development so putting them together seemed obvious! I go to concerts all the time and make playlists so that I go in prepared. With this application, you can save yourself hours of adding individual songs to a playlist for that upcoming concert, or you can relive a recent show by making a playlist out of that! To use:

  1. Log into your Spotify account with the Login button in the menu bar.
  2. Search for an artist.
  3. Choose a setlist.
  4. Name your playlist and choose whether it should be public or private
  5. Open up Spotify and enjoy!
